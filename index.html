Honkai Star Rail Review

Honkai Star Rail is on of the best turn based gatcha games out in 2023.
The story starts incredibly strong instantly hooking the player in.
It's turn based gameplay is a nice change of pace with all of open world games out right now.
Each character has there own background and unique personality which helps to build connects to the players.
